My Projects

Plexi-gig model platform

Made with:

Plexi is a gig model platform that connects Helpers and Help Needers, facilitating assistance with everyday tasks such as lawn mowing, cleaning, and moving. It features advanced security measures, including face recognition, emergency assistance, and background checks, ensuring a safe and trustworthy environment. With a user-friendly interface and efficient matching system, Plexi makes it easy for Help Needers to find reliable help and for Helpers to find rewarding gigs. Committed to supporting those in need and empowering Helpers, Plexi fosters a community of care and support.
